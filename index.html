Bridging the digital divide by teaching code to the next generation

Our Mission

CodeBridge empowers underrepresented and younger students by teaching them foundational coding and technology skills through free workshops and mentorship.

What We Offer

  • Beginner-friendly weekly coding classes (Scratch, Python)
  • Fun and interactive projects
  • Workshops hosted online or at local libraries/schools
  • Volunteer mentorship opportunities for high school students
